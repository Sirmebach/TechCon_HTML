History

TechCon began about seven years ago as a small meetup for technology enthusiasts called Hackathon. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of attendees annually to explore the latest advancements in technology.

Mission

Our mission is to connect innovators, inspire growth, and foster collaboration among tech enthusiasts and professionals worldwide. We believe in the power of technology to shape the future for the better.
